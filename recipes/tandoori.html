Tandoori Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

For Marinade:

  • 4 chicken legs or pieces (with bone)
  • 1 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1-2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves, optional)

To prepare the Tandoori Chicken, start by making the marinade. In a bowl, combine yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, oil, and salt. If you have kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves), crush it and add to the marinade. Coat the chicken pieces well with the marinade and let it sit for at least 2 hours (or overnight for more flavor) in the refrigerator. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the marinated chicken on a baking rack and bake for 30-40 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred. Optionally, baste the chicken with oil or butter halfway through. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, rice, or a side of salad.
